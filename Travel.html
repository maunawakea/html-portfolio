Top 5 Places I Want to Visit

Place Country Why Budget (excl. plane tickets)
Glenfinnan Viaduct UK One of the most iconic locations from the Harry Potter film series, and the longest concrete railway bridge in Scotland. In summer, visitors have an opportunity to ride on the real-life Hogwarts Express, by booking a 6-hour round-trip on the Jacobite steam train. ~500€ for 2 nights + tour+ various expenses
Black Park UK Besides being depicted as the Forbidden Forest, the park's majestic woodland has served as a set for many well-known films and TV series of the 20th and 21th century, such as the Hammer horror productions, James Bond, Jurassic Park, Star Wars, the MCU, and Doctor Who (Wikipedia) ~400€ for 2 nights+ transportation + various expenses
Freshwater West,Pembrokeshire National Park UK Filming location for both Deathly Hollows films and the site of Dobby's grave, which was kept intact after the filming was concluded. The beach itself is part of a picturesque hiking trail across the coastline. ~600€ for 2 nights + transportation + various expenses
York UK Founded during the Roman imperial period, the city has one of the highest concentrations of historical monuments and landmarks in the country. The Shambles, one of the most well-preserved medieval streets in England, is widely believed to have been the inspiration for the Wizarding World's Diagon Alley. ~700€ for 7 nights + transportation + various expenses
Lavenham Village UK Britain's best preserved medieval village, it served as a filming location for scenes set in the Godric's Hollow, one of the most important locations in the entire story. It is also an excellent starting point for exploring the English countryside on foot, as Lavenham is connected to the other four Suffolk wool towns by an established circular path. ~600€ for 2 nights+transportation+various expenses
